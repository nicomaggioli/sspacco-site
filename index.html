03 — about

A little about me
(I like over-sharing).

BFA in Illustration from Massachusetts College of Art and Design. Since school I've been collaboratively designing and illustrating for brands including Bodega, Wilson, Franklin, Converse, and Feels Like Home.

I also work as a freelance artist for local musicians and other talents — making promotional flyers and cover art.
